# Vision

## What is Wisteria?

Wisteria is a pocket e-reader built with love for Leah. It is designed to last a lifetime and make reading simple, peaceful, and personal.

## Primary purpose

**Reading comes first.** Every design decision should serve the core experience of sitting down with a book and turning pages without friction.

Personalization features are welcome, but they must remain **secondary and lightweight**. They should enhance the reading experience, not compete with it.

## Design principles

- **Simple** — Fewer taps, fewer menus, fewer distractions.
- **Peaceful** — A calm device for calm reading; E Ink supports this naturally.
- **Personal** — Built for Leah, with room for small touches that make it hers.
- **Lasting** — Hardware and software choices should favor durability and maintainability over novelty.

## What Wisteria is not

- A general-purpose tablet or media player
- A platform for heavy customization or app ecosystems
- A device where polish and personality overshadow the book

## Audience

Wisteria has one reader: Leah. Features and scope should be judged against whether they genuinely improve her reading life.
